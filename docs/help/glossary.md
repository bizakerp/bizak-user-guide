# Glossary

This page will define the business terms used throughout Bizak documentation.

## Starter terms

- **Customer**: a party that buys goods or services
- **Vendor**: a party that supplies goods or services
- **Ledger**: a financial account used for accounting
- **Item**: a sellable or purchasable good or service
- **Role**: the permissions assigned to a user
- **Opportunity**: a sales lead that is being tracked
- **Estimate**: a quote or proposal for a customer
- **Purchase order**: a request to buy goods or services from a vendor
- **Item receipt**: a record that goods were received
- **Journal entry**: a direct accounting entry
- **Opening balance**: the starting balance for a ledger
- **Trial balance**: a report that compares debits and credits
- **Inventory adjustment**: a correction to stock on hand
