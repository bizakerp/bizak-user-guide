# FAQs

Frequently asked questions should use short answers.

## Common questions

| Question | Short answer |
| --- | --- |
| How do I reset access for a user? | Update the user or role mapping in administration. |
| Why can’t I post this transaction? | Check setup, required fields, and approval state. |
| Why is a field disabled? | The field may depend on role or configuration. |
| How do I find a report? | Open the Reports section and choose the correct family. |
| Why does a ledger not balance? | Check the line amounts and opening balances. |
| Why is a record missing from the list? | Confirm the date, filters, branch, and access rights. |
