# Sales

The Sales module supports customer-facing commercial workflows.

## Typical sales workflows

- opportunity
- estimate
- sales order
- fulfillment
- sales invoice
- credit memo
- return or adjustment

## Example flow

1. Create an opportunity
2. Convert it into an estimate
3. Approve or revise the estimate
4. Fulfill the order
5. Generate a sales invoice
6. Receive customer payment
7. Review receivables in reports
