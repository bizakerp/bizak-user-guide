# POS Payment

This page covers the POS payment screen.
Use it when the sale is ready to close.

## Before you start

- Confirm the items are already on the POS sale.
- Confirm the payment method is ready.
- Confirm the cashier has session access.
- Confirm the POS setup matches the store.

## What the screen does

- It collects payment for the POS sale.
- It finalizes the order.
- It supports the payment flow after items are entered.

## Example

The cashier finishes the item scan and opens payment.
The system records the amount and completes the sale.
