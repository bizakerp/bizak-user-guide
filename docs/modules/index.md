# Modules

Bizak modules are grouped by business function.

## Start here

If you want the main workflows first, open these pages:

<div class="grid cards landing-grid" markdown>

-   :material-cart-outline: **Sales**

    Track leads, orders, invoices, and customer adjustments.

    [Open page](sales.md)

-   :material-cart-outline: **Procurement**

    Handle supplier quotes, orders, receipts, and bills.

    [Open page](procurement.md)

-   :material-warehouse: **Inventory**

    Move stock, adjust quantities, and review expiry.

    [Open page](inventory.md)

-   :material-cash-register: **POS**

    Run counter sales, sessions, and payment flows.

    [Open page](pos.md)

-   :material-bank: **Finance**

    Post journals, budgets, opening balances, and expense reports.

    [Open page](finance.md)

</div>

## Before you choose a module

- Decide which business team owns the workflow.
- Confirm the required setup pages are ready.
- Use the guided journeys when you want a role-based path.
